Laptop cooling stands and risers do more than just keep your machine from overheating: they improve ergonomics by lifting the screen to eye level, reduce neck strain, and boost airflow under the chassis. A good budget model can cost a fraction of a premium stand while still offering solid construction, adjustable angles, and enough ventilation to keep fans from spinning up under load. When shopping on a budget, focus on build materials (aluminum vs. plastic), adjustability (tilt and height range), and portability if you move between desks or rooms. You don’t need a built-in fan for most office or browsing tasks – a well-ventilated passive stand is often lighter, quieter, and more durable.
